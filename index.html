Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I use the app to identify a plant?
    A: Simply open the app, take a clear photo of the plant you want to identify, and our AI will analyze it and provide you with the most likely matches.
  • Q: Are there in-app purchases?
    A: Yes, PlantIQ offers in-app purchases for premium features to enhance your plant identification experience.
  • Q: How accurate is the plant identification?
    A: Our AI is highly accurate, but results may vary depending on the quality of the photo and the uniqueness of the plant. We're constantly improving our algorithms for better accuracy.
  • Q: Does the app store my photos?
    A: No, PlantIQ does not store any of your photos. Images are processed in real-time and immediately discarded after identification.
